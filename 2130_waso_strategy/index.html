Academy

Switchly Transformer Academy is a 5-day intensive training boot camp designed to introduce Entrepreneurs with just an idea or existing busines to the startup world. It helps them embrace the startup mindset, culture, principles and strategies with a view to positioning them for growth and investment readiness. The training is facilitated by an experienced faculty of resource persons mostly Startup founders themselves and other relevant professionals.

The Academy Caters for 2 categories of entrepreneurs:

  1. People with ideas that need validation and training on how to transform their ideas into investment-ready startups via the program Idea2Startup and,

  2. Existing small business owners that need to audit their existing business structure, culture and processes and need help in transforming their businesses into investment-ready startups viathe Biz2Startup program.
